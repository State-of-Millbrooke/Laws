<h1>Employment Standards Act</h1>

*Chapter -, Statutes of Millbrooke*

**Enacted - Month 202-**
  
</div align="center">

---
## INTERPRETATION AND APPLICATION

1.	This Act applies to all employees and employers, including those covered by the *Public Service Employment Act*.

## EMPLOYEE RIGHTS

2.	No person may be denied employment except for good cause.
3.	No person may be punished or retaliated against for reporting misconduct, harassment, abuse, or any unlawful act.
4.	No person may be subject to oppression, coercion, or obstruction during the course of their employment.
5.	No person may be discriminated against, in the context of their employment and in any respect connected to their employment, on the basis of any of the following:

- Their choice of the model of their body, including the usage of any torsos that includes characteristics from either sex; or
- Their choice of hair colour, unless the choice is not a naturally occurring hair colour.

6.	Every person has the right to access their own employment records and make corrections to any incorrect information on those records.

## REMEDY

7.	Anyone whose rights, as provided in this Act, have been infringed or denied may apply to a court of competent jurisdiction to obtain such remedy as the court considers appropriate and just in the circumstances.
