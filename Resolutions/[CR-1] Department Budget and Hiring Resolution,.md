<h1>Department Budget and Hiring Resolution</h1>

*Chapter 1, Council Resolutions*

**Effective 29 April 2026**
  
</div align="center">

---

**WHEREAS,** budgets are crucial in the provisional setup of a government to keep the game balanced and realistic for release; 

**WHEREAS,** no legislative body is active and able to set restrictions for these departments; 

**NOW, THEREFORE,** the Executive Council enacts as follows:

1.	Until otherwise provided by the Executive Council, a department named in Column A must not exceed its budget provided in Column B:

| Column A | Column B |
| --- | --- |
| Millbrooke Bureau of Investigation| 15 employees|
| Hennepin County Sheriff’s Office| 50 commissioned employees|
| Beecher Police Department| 45 commissioned employees|
| Millbrooke National Guard| 65 non-commissioned officers plus 35 commissioned officers|
| Hennepin County Fire Department| 30 employees|
| Millbrooke Law Enforcement Academy| 20 employees|
| Millbrooke Department of Justice| 20 employees|
| Millbrooke Department of State| 20 employees|

2. Until game release or otherwise provided by the Executive Council, a department named in Column A must appoint all of its employees at the rank and level provided in Column B, except for employees appointed to a rank or level or position which is equivalent to or higher than that provided in Column C:

| Column A | Column B | Column C |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hennepin County Sheriff’s Office| Cadet | Lieutenant |
| Beecher Police Department| Police Cadet | Lieutenant |
| Millbrooke National Guard| Private | Captain, a commissioned officer, or Military Police personnel |
| Hennepin County Fire Department| 30 employees| Fire Candidate | Captain |

3. The appointment of every employee in the Millbrooke Department of Justice, except for Justice Staffers, is conditional on that employee being granted a license to practice law from the Bar within a reasonable time after the Bar becomes operational or begins administering licenses to practice law.

4.	For the purposes of this document, a “commissioned officer” is defined as a member of the Millbrooke National Guard that meets the following criteria:
- A Judge Advocate General
- A member of the Military Police Brigade
- Any member, regardless of brigade, ranked a Major or higher.
